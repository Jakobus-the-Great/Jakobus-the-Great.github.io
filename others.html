Associate Members

Associate members are Nordic Council members who are only afforded two representatives in the council. They are free to adopt council resolutions should their legislatures decide to do so. The associate members consist of Greenland, the Aland Islands, and the Faroe Islands.

Observer Members

Observer members have no representation in the council, but may be seeking future membership. Their populations are largely Scandinavian, thus their participation in the council. The observer members are the Baltic countries of Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania, as well as the German state of Schleswig-Holstein.
